Physical and Mental Health at UMSI

At the University of Michigan, we are deeply committed to fostering the physical and mental well-being of our students through a comprehensive range of health and wellness resources. University Health Service (UHS) offers convenient access to medical care, including primary care, vaccinations, and specialized services, while the Counseling and Psychological Services (CAPS) team provides confidential mental health support through individual counseling, group therapy, and wellness workshops. For physical fitness and recreation, students can take advantage of state-of-the-art facilities, including gyms, pools, and outdoor recreational spaces, as well as a variety of fitness classes and intramural sports. Additionally, wellness initiatives like mindfulness programs, peer support groups, and stress management resources are designed to help students build resilience and balance in their daily lives. By utilizing these resources, students at the University of Michigan can prioritize their health and well-being, enabling them to thrive both academically and personally.
